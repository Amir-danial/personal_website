    • Review

    CS2 is... well, it's a bit of a mixed bag. On one hand, it looks amazing—way cleaner and more modern than CS:GO. The new smokes are super cool, and the gunplay still feels tight and satisfying. But let’s be real: the launch was kinda rough. A lot of stuff we loved from CS:GO was just missing, and it felt like we were beta testing instead of playing a finished game. Some bugs were annoying, and matchmaking didn’t always feel fair. Still, when it does work, it’s a blast, especially with friends. It’s not perfect, but it’s heading in the right direction. So yeah, 4 out of 5—it’s good, just needs a bit more time to fully shine.
