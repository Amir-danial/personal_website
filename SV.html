    • Review

    Stardew Valley is super charming and has a ton of heart—you can really feel the love that went into making it. The pixel art, the music, the peaceful farm life vibe—it’s all really cozy. But for me, it started to drag after a while. The early game is fun, planting crops and slowly building up your farm, but then it kind of turns into a grind. Days can start feeling repetitive, and if you're not super into managing a schedule or chasing relationship events, it can get a little boring. I get why so many people love it, and I respect what it is, but personally, it didn’t fully hook me. It’s good, just not great—for me at least. Solid 3 out of 5.
