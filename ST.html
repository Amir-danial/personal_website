    • Review

    Stacklands had a cool concept that caught my attention—combining card-based gameplay with village building sounded like a fun, chill strategy experience. And at first, it kinda is. The art is simple and charming, and stacking cards to manage resources feels fresh. But the fun wears off pretty fast. The pacing can get frustrating, especially when you start juggling too many things with too little space, and the randomness of card drops makes it feel more like luck than strategy. After a while, it just gets repetitive and a bit chaotic in a not-so-fun way. It’s a neat idea, but it doesn’t really hold up long-term. I wanted to like it more, but honestly, it felt more tedious than enjoyable. So yeah, 2 out of 5 for me.
