Hi! My name is Amir Danial

I'm currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Media Communication Technology at UiTM Alor Gajah. This website is part of a personal assignment where I was tasked with creating a site about one of my hobbies — and I chose gaming. I’ve featured five of my favourite games, each chosen for their unique style and impact on me as a player. Everything you see on this website was made entirely from scratch — from the layout and design to the custom video background, which I personally created. It was important for me to make this project reflect not just my interests, but also my creative abilities. As you explore, you’ll also find a list of software tools I’m skilled in, showcasing what I’ve learned so far in my journey as a digital media student.
