    • Review

    LEGO Marvel Super Heroes 2 is just plain fun. It doesn’t take itself too seriously, which makes it perfect if you’re just looking to relax and mess around in a big, chaotic Marvel universe. The roster of characters is huge, and playing as everyone from Spider-Man 2099 to Baby Groot is a blast. The humor is cheesy in the best way, and the puzzles are simple but satisfying. That said, the combat can feel a bit repetitive, and sometimes the camera angles or controls get a little clunky, especially in co-op. But honestly, the game’s charm kind of makes up for it. It’s one of those games that doesn’t try to be groundbreaking, just genuinely entertaining. A solid 4 out of 5—great for Marvel fans or anyone who just wants to smash stuff with LEGO bricks.
